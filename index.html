What
I do

I work across AI product design and agentic UX , translating opaque machine behaviour into interfaces people can understand and trust. I complement that with end-to-end product design, design systems, and UX research grounded in real user feedback and data. My interaction and visual design background gives me a strong sense of hierarchy, motion, and detail.
